The Heritage

Born in Mthwalume on the scenic South Coast of KwaZulu-Natal, Niipseedadust's complex musical alignment was forged and completely matured within KwaMashu, Durban. This transition from raw coastal frequencies to one of South Africa's most historic urban club environments outlines his signature rhythms.
